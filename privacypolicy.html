Privacy Policy

This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for www.ExpressCashFlow.com.
Quick Commission Advance recognizes and respects the importance of maintaining the privacy of our customers and our website’s users. Accordingly, we created this privacy statement to disclose Quick Commission Advance’s information gathering and dissemination practices.

Information Collection and Use
You can browse through Quick Commission Advance’s website without telling us who you are or revealing any information about yourself. However, if you want to use our services, you must fill-out the contact form or email us by providing personal information.

We only use your personal information to help us service your account, to improve our services to you, to provide you with payment of your real estate commissions on a factored basis, and to inform you about any promotions that may be of interest to you. We will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. In the event that court orders or laws mandate Quick Commission Advance to disclose personal information, we will do so only to the extent required by law.

Opting in to Subscriber List
By clicking this box your are opting into our subscriber list.  You may or may not receive communication from Quick Commission Advance in the future.

Notification of Changes
If we update our privacy policy, we will post a notice on our Home page www.ExpressCashFlow.com). Anyone can review the changes by going directly to our privacy statement. This ensures readers are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. If at any point we decide to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated in our privacy policy at the time it was collected, we will notify users by way of an email. We will offer users a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner.

Cookies
A cookie is information passed from a web server to a web browser. Quick Commission Advance’s website uses cookies to maintain the integrity of a client login session. No personal information is passed or stored in the cookie.

Log Files
We use Internet Protocol (IP) addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track users’ movements, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Sharing
We are the sole owners of our customer list. We do not sell, rent, or share personally identifiable demographic information with our partners, affiliates or advertisers.

Security
The Quick Commission Advance website uses high-grade, 128 bit key encryption. You will notice the security lock next to our URL. Any information you enter is safe and secure. Quick Commission Advance takes every precaution to protect our users’ information both online and off-line. The servers on which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment. We also ensure our staff’s trustworthiness. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job function have access to personally identifiable information.

Supplementation of Information
In order for Quick Commission Advance to properly fulfill its obligation to our customers, it is necessary for us to independently verify each sale contract with the attorney, escrow company or title company that is closing the sale. We do this by calling the contact name on the customer’s sale contract. We tell the contact we are funding a portion of the commission on the sale and ask for their confirmation that they have opened escrow and are acting as the closing entity. We never contact buyers or sellers, nor share any personal information.

Special Offers
Established customers or prospective customers will occasionally receive information on promotions whereby we offer price saving on certain products and services. Out of respect for the privacy of our customers, we will never sell, share, transfer or disclose our customer list in any other way. We also present our customers the option to not receive these types of communications.

Consumer Recourse
If you feel that this company is not abiding by its posted privacy policy, please contact Calvin Swartz the Managing Partner, Calvin@QuickCommission.com.
